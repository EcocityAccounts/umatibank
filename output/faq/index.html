Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I start a group in Umati Bank?   
  • New groups can register directly here on the website.  Out admins,  Kiama, Ria and Geog are available to assist you through the process and they are available from the moment you sign up. Claudia Mbina Mwende (Mbindamwende@yahoo.co.ke) and Daniel Murithi (artatheart3839@gmail.com) are available to assist your group should your group require professional support or trainings.
  • Can a group collaborate with other stake holders?
  • the Umati Bank provides assistance for groups to draft constitutions, MOU agreements, etc. Defining their relationship relative to NGOs, private investors and government institutions.  We provide connections to Kiva fund and Equity Bank foundation, but believe that it is smart to grow sustainably, build healthy small business dynamics through steady regular participation rather than following the traditional route of inferfacing with debt culture. We are a comunity that embraces a culture of savings and the benefits of mutual support and solidarity, so we encourage your group to honor one another and work to replace traditional bank loans, even if they are offered at 0% as we have seen from Equity Fund
  • Can a group register without a registration certificate?

  • Yes, one can register in Umatibank directly and begin the process of establishing your group be it a CBO, self help group, youth group, womens group, etc.  

  • can mens groups' be registered?
  •  yes
  • How many members can register via Umatibank?
  •  Group size is ideally between 10 and 30 per group, but we are willing to make exceptions of you request a different option.
  • What are the benefits after joining?
  • Comunity banking is not a new concept and has been used to pool resources among trusted members of community for many years in many countries. We find that by having regular meetings where members of small groups support one another through the challenges of lifting themselves up into a better economic flow pattern, a culture of savings is created that has the strength to carry members through challenging times. We wish you the best and encourage you to focus on celebrating and recognizing each of the small successes so that the larger goal can be realized in a collaborative, low stress manner.
