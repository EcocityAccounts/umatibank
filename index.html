Umati is uniquely dedicated to true regenerative benefits to communities.  UmatiBank platform aims to provide a secure, transparent, self managed revolving loan fund system for small communities working toward self sufficiency.  Umati Bank, in conjunction with educational non-profit SCD Hub are currently working with a self help group of 24 people in Njiru Kenya operating as "Art at Heart". 

Many projects that focus on community development bring with them very traditional approaches to their models for what a 'healthy economy' is.  Umati Bank is not such a project. Our work responds to deeply rooted systemic problems within society's very wiring.
