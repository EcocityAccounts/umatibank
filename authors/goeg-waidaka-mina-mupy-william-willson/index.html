Frequently Asked Questions Yes, one can register in Umatibank directly and begin the process of establishing your group be it a CBO, self help group, youth group, womens group, etc. can mens groups' be registered? yesHow many members can register via Umatibank? Group size is ideally between 10 and 30 per group, but we are willing to make exceptions of you request a different option.What are the benefits after joining?Comunity banking is not a new concept and has been used to pool resources among trusted members of community for many years in many countries. We find that by having regular meetings where members of small groups support one another through the challenges of lifting themselves up into a better economic flow pattern, a culture of savings is created that has the strength to carry members through challenging times. We wish you the best and encourage you to focus on celebrating and recognizing each of the small successes so that the larger goal can be realized in a collaborative, low stress manner.
